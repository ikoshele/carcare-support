Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. When you are signed in to iCloud, it is also synced to your other devices through Apple CloudKit's private database. CarCare has no developer-operated servers and never sends your information to us or any third party.

How do I back up my data?

If iCloud sync is active, your CarCare data is automatically kept in your iCloud account and available on all your devices. Your data is also included in standard iPhone backups (iCloud Backup or Finder/iTunes backup).

Can I transfer data to a new phone?

Yes — if you are signed in to the same iCloud account on your new iPhone, your CarCare data will sync automatically. Alternatively, restoring from an iPhone backup also transfers all your data.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual vehicles, records, or fuel logs within the app. Uninstalling the app removes data stored on that device. To also remove data from iCloud, go to Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage and remove CarCare data from there.
